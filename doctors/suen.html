Dentistry for me is my work but it is also a passion. Having graduated from University of Sydney in 2019, which focus on “bringing the mouth back to health” philosophy emphasizing oral health being part of general medicine, I believed that my work in the field of dentistry can benefit a patient’s general health. As many of my patients, I am fearful of any dental treatment due to past dental experience. Therefore, I honed my skill towards pain-free dentistry during my DMD program and focus on caring about how the patient feel during dental procedure. I enjoy working with kids because we all need to have good habit of good oral hygiene and not be fearful of dentist. This is what I work towards and you can expect me to take care of my patient as I take care of my family. I enjoy volunteering and was involved with McGill Dental Outreach to volunteer from before my DMD program. I strongly believe that educating our patient about good dental hygiene and focusing more on prevention rather than intervention; hence I tend to speak more to my patient on oral hygiene and am happy to answer questions from patients.
