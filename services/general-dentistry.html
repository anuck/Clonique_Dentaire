Emergency Services

We try to treat your dental emergencies in less than 24 hours to relieve your pain whether it is severe dental pain caused by a broken tooth or any other dental emergency.

Extended hours and open on weekends, our dentists are specifically available to manage your emergencies making it THE dental office for your dental emergencies less than 24 hours in the greater Montreal Region.

Dental pain prevents you from enjoying good times with your family or friends. This pain prevents you from getting to work or preventing you from sleeping at night, don't delay it further! An emergency dentist under 24 hours is possible! Contact us and our receptionists will be pleased to give you an appointment.

Tooth Colored Fillings

Composite resins, or tooth-colored fillings, provide good durability and resistance to fracture in small- to mid-size fillings that need to withstand moderate pressure from the constant stress of chewing.

Extractions

An extraction means to have a tooth removed, usually because of disease, trauma or crowding. This is a common procedure and the area of the extraction is anesthesized for the removal of a tooth.

Root Canals

Root canal treatment is necessary when soft tissue inside your teeth becomes inflamed or diseased. During root canal treatment, the diseased area is effectively removed and treated. Afterward, the root canals and the pulp chamber are filled and sealed. Although the pulp and root canals are vital for the growth and development of the tooth, a fully matured tooth can still survive without them.

Digital Radiographs

Dental X-rays are a useful diagnostic tool to detect damage and disease not visible during a dental exam. Use of X-rays depends on present oral health, your age, your risk for disease, and any signs of oral disease.
